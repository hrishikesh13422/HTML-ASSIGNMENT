My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Drishyam
    • Year: 2013
    • Main Character: Georgekutty (played by Mohanlal)
    • Description: A gripping thriller about a man who goes to great lengths to protect his family after a crime.
  2. Kumbalangi Nights
    • Year: 2019
    • Main Character: Saji (played by Fahadh Faasil)
    • Description: A beautifully crafted tale of four brothers and their struggles, set against the backdrop of a picturesque village.
  3. Bangalore Days
    • Year: 2014
    • Main Character: Arjun (played by Dulquer Salmaan)
    • Description: A coming-of-age story about three cousins who navigate love and life in Bangalore.
  4. Maheshinte Prathikaaram
    • Year: 2016
    • Main Character: Mahesh (played by Fahadh Faasil)
    • Description: A light-hearted comedy-drama about a photographer's quest for revenge after being humiliated.
  5. Gadha
    • Year: 2016
    • Main Character: Karthik (played by Asif Ali)
    • Description: A story of friendship and personal growth as a young man navigates the challenges of life.
