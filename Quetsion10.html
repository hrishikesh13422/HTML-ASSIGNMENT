Using Special Characters

In HTML, some characters have special meanings,some of them are:

  • Less than: < (<)
  • Greater than: > (>)
  • Ampersand: & (&)
  • Copyright: © (©)
  • Registered trademark: ® (®)
  • Trademark: ™ (™)
